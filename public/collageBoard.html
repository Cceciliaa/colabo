Welcome to CollageBoard-3D!

This is a place for online group collaboration. Add texts, images, and 3D models to your collage by clicking on the plus icon on the upper left corner.

All elements are added to the board as "element cards", and all elements on the board are shared among the group members in real time.


How to use?

Add text/annotation: Add a "text" element and type in the textarea.

Add an image: Add an "image" element, then clicking on the small "+" button at the upper-left corner of the card to open the interface for file upload.

Upload an Image File: Upload your images from local device, URLs, Web search, or pulling from other mainstream media platform.

Add a 3D Model: Add a "3D Model" element, then clicking on the small "+" button to search for and choose a 3D model to be loaded.

Start/Stop rotating a 3D Model: Click on anywhere inside the "3D Model" element card.


Move the Card to a different position: drag the card around.

Bring current selection to the top: double click on the card.

Delete a card: click on the "x" icon at the upper-left corner of the card.


Click anywhere to resume your work!

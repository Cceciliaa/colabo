Welcome to CollageBoard-3D!

This is a place for online group collaboration. Add texts, images, and 3D models by clicking on the plus icon on the upper left corner.

All elements are added to the board as "element cards", and all elements on the board are shared among the group members in real time.


How to use?

Add text/annotation: Add a "text" element and type in the textarea.

Add a 3D Model: Add a "3D Model" element. To refresh your choice for 3D models, click on the small "+" button.

Add an image: Add an "image" element, then clicking on the small "+" button at the upper-left corner of the card to open the interface for file upload.

Bring an element to front: double click on the element card.

Delete an element: click on the "x" icon at the upper-left corner of the element card.

Click anywhere to get to your work!

Why Colabo?

• Unlimited canvas and multiple elements.

• Easy access to abundant open-source 3D models.

• Cloud saving and instant sharing.

• More engaging online collaboration!

How to Use?

• Open a board or create a new one.

• Decide a title for the board.

• Create your collage with texts, images, and 3D models.

• Response and add to the collage made by your team-mates.
